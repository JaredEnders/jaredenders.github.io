Analyzing Weather Patterns in Relation to Latitude

This page allows the viewer to analyze the relationship between certain weather patterns and the latitude of particular cities around the world.

The metrics of temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed were all plotted against the latitudes from hundreds of cities and can help our viewers draw more conclusive trends, and extract meaningful data from the graphs.
